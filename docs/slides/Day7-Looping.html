Assemblages

  • Contests the idea that data are hard, shiny objects that can be held, acquired, etc.
  • When we talk about data, we are actually referring to many interrelated things:
    • Materialities, practices, subjectivities, places, governmentalities, etc.

  • “Data” emerges in the wake of these interrelated assemblages
